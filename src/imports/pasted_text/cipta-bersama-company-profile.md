Design a **premium, modern, and visually engaging company profile website** for a meat supplier company named **Cipta Bersama**.

The website targets **B2B clients such as restaurants, hotels, catering businesses, and cafes**, therefore the design must feel **professional, trustworthy, and high-end**.

The visual quality should be **better and more modern than the Indoguna website**, with stronger product presentation and more refined UI.

---

DESIGN GOAL

Create a website that communicates:

• Premium meat products
• Professional distribution
• Reliable supplier for restaurants and hotels
• Strong brand credibility

The website should feel like a **high-end food distributor brand**.

---

VISUAL STYLE

Style direction:

• Modern corporate website
• Premium food supplier aesthetic
• Clean layout with strong visual hierarchy
• Large product photography
• Elegant blue gradients and curved shapes
• Minimal but visually rich design

Avoid clutter. Focus on **strong imagery and clear sections**.

---

COLOR SYSTEM

Use colors based on the Cipta Bersama logo.

Primary color: Corporate Blue
Secondary color: Light Blue Gradient
Accent color: Deep Navy Blue

Background:
White or very light gray for clean appearance.

Use **soft gradient shapes and subtle abstract curves** to enhance visual depth.

---

TYPOGRAPHY

Use modern sans-serif typography.

Headlines:
Bold and strong.

Subheadings:
Medium weight.

Body text:
Clean and readable.

Hierarchy should clearly separate:

• section titles
• descriptions
• product labels

---

GRID SYSTEM

Desktop frame: 1440px

Content width: 1200px
Grid: 12 column layout

Spacing system:

Small spacing: 16px
Medium spacing: 32px
Large spacing: 80px

Sections should feel **spacious and breathable**.

---

UI COMPONENT STYLE

Buttons:

Rounded corners
Soft shadow
Primary color background
Hover animation

Cards:

Rounded corners
Soft shadows
Hover elevation effect

Images:

Large product photography
High contrast and appetizing food images

Icons:

Minimal line icons.

---

INTERACTION DESIGN

Add subtle modern micro-interactions:

• Product cards zoom slightly on hover
• Buttons glow or elevate on hover
• Smooth scroll transitions
• Section fade-in animations on scroll
• Parallax effect in hero section

These interactions should be **subtle and professional**.

---
